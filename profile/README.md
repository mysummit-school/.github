# 👋 Welcome to mysummit.school

**Practical AI for managers — from basics to real-world application.**

We are an independent online school that teaches managers to use Generative AI (ChatGPT, Claude, YandexGPT, DeepSeek, and more) through real, hands-on cases — not by collecting abstract prompts.

🌐 **[mysummit.school](https://mysummit.school)** · 📚 **[Open the textbook](https://open.mysummit.school)** · ✉️ welcome@mysummit.school

---

## What we do

We help managers move from *"I've heard about AI"* to *"I use it every day, safely, and it saves me hours."* The curriculum is built by practicing managers for practicing managers — every case comes from real people-management and project-management work.

### Why we're different

- 🛠 **Practice over theory** — we teach *how to apply* AI, detect hallucinations, and verify answers. No prompt galleries, no hype.
- 🎯 **Role-based tracks** — a shared Foundation module plus four specializations: **People Management**, **Project Management**, **Education** (L&D), and **Technical Depth**.
- 🌍 **Built for the Russian market** — adapted for a Russian-speaking audience, including tools that work without a VPN.
- 🔬 **Grounded in research** — the program draws on work from MIT, Stanford, and Microsoft, plus our own [benchmark of 54 AI models](https://mysummit.school/research/benchmark/).
- 🔄 **Updated weekly** — the tooling landscape moves fast; so do we.

> *Учимся сами, набиваем шишки за вас. Учим применять, а не тешить самомнение новыми знаниями.*
> We learn ourselves, make mistakes for you. We teach application, not vanity.

### How you learn

Text-based lessons (~20 min each) with embedded checkpoints, interactive assignments, AI-graded tasks, and quizzes. Fully self-paced and 100% online. Start with the **9-lesson open module — free, no registration required**.
